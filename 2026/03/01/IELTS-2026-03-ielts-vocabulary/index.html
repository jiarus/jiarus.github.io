IELTS 备考笔记 | 3月

📚 3月单词、短语、句型积累

Week 1 | 主题：教育 Education

📖 核心词汇

单词 词性 释义 例句
curriculum n 课程体系 The school has updated its curriculum.
compulsory adj 强制的，必修的 English is a compulsory subject.
tuition n 学费；教学 Tuition fees have risen sharply.
literacy n 读写能力 Improving adult literacy is essential.
dropout n 辍学者 The dropout rate has decreased.

🔄 表达升级

❌ 基础表达 ✅ 高分替换
important crucial / vital / essential / paramount
more and more an increasing number of / a growing proportion of
very good excellent / outstanding / exceptional
help facilitate / contribute to / foster
bad detrimental / adverse / harmful
think maintain / argue / contend / hold the view that
students learn students acquire knowledge / develop skills
have problems encounter difficulties / face challenges
because due to / owing to / on account of
so consequently / as a result / therefore

✍️ 好句积累

Education is not merely about acquiring knowledge, but about fostering critical thinking and creativity.
教育不仅仅是获取知识，更是培养批判性思维和创造力。

It is widely acknowledged that equal access to quality education can bridge the gap between different social classes.
人们普遍认为，平等获得优质教育可以缩小不同社会阶层之间的差距。

❌ 错误拆解

写作常见错误

错误 正确 说明
The education is important. Education is important. 抽象概念不加 the
Many students wants… Many students want… 主谓一致
It has many benefits, such as improve… …such as improving… such as 后接名词/动名词

阅读踩坑

  • 看到 NOT GIVEN 和 FALSE 总是混淆 → 记住：NG 是”文中没提”，FALSE 是”文中说了相反的”

🧠 Meta 思考

这周发现自己写作时总想用中文思维直译，比如”提高学生的能力”写成 “improve students’ ability”，其实更地道的说法是 “enhance students’ competence” 或 “develop students’ capability”。下周要刻意练习先想英文表达框架，再填内容。
